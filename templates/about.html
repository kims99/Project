About this Database

There are hundreds of vendors each year who join us at the Iowa State Fair. At the Communications Department, we receive vendors' orders early in order to help prepare them at the State Fair. The purpose of this database is to easily view and search through our organized database for existing order information. Our department has the abilities to add, update, and remove orders, while other departments are only able to view and search the orders.

If you have any questions or there is any update to a vendor's order, please go to the contact page.
